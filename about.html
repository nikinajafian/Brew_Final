Our Story

Our brand is honest and passionate about providing our customers with clean and fresh ingredients in every bar. It is important to us that our protein bars and their packagings are 100% vegan and sustainable. Our bars have a tea or coffee base which can easily be a healthy snack replacement for the energy boost provided by a cup of tea of coffee throughout the day. We offer a selection of flavoursâ€”for tea we have Earl Grey, Matcha, Hibiscus, Dragonwell, English Breakfast and Chamomile; and for coffee we have Espresso, Mocha and Caramel Macchiato.

Our bars contain oats as the main protein which are rich in antioxidants and are a great source of fiber. We use agave as a sweetener instead of honey to make sure every aspect is kept cruelty-free and vegan. The different bars we offer contain a variety of ingredients, many of which include a mix of nuts and seeds. To be inclusive of individuals with allergies, we produce three nut-free options.

The bars are wrapped with soy wax and placed carefully in small compostable boxes. The soy wax wraps are reusable for as long as 6-12 months with regular and proper use. This provides customers with yet another way to help the environment with each purchase. The compostable boxes ensure that the individually wrapped bars stay well contained without using unnecessary plastic or foil. We are extremely committed to providing organic, plant-based solutions for healthy living. As such, our focus starts with you and extends into the environment. Our packaging is an example of our commitment to sustainability beyond human health. In addition to being a company devoted to the protection of the environment, we also highly value great design. For this reason, each soy wax wrap for a given flavour features a unique creative design that will encourage people to show off their contribution to the environment and a vegan lifestyle.
